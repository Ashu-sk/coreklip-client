How it works

Three steps.
Zero editing skills needed.

Built for Indian creators who have great content but not hours to edit it.

STEP 01 🎙️

Paste your video link

YouTube, podcast, Instagram Live, Google Drive — CoreClip pulls in the full video automatically. No uploading, no waiting.

STEP 02 ✂️

Our model detects viral hooks, emotional peaks, and quotable insights. Get 5–15 clips ready with captions in Hindi, English, or your regional language.

One click sends your clips to Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Moj, Josh, LinkedIn, and X — automatically, on your schedule.
